Results & Performance

98.9% Fire detection accuracy
99.62% License plate detection accuracy
~1% Average false detection

The fire detection system was evaluated across varied lighting and motion conditions; the pipeline emphasizes early smoke/heat recognition to maximize early response while minimizing false alarms.
